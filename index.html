Study Medicine in Romania

Explore the benefits of pursuing a medical degree in Romania

Program Offerings

Romanian universities offer various medical programs including General Medicine, Dentistry, and Pharmacy.

Affordable Tuition Fees

Romania is more cost-effective, with relatively lower tuition fees, making it an attractive option for international students.

Degrees Awarded

Upon successful completion of the program, students are awarded internationally recognized degrees such as MD, DDS, or PharmD.

English-Taught Programs

Many universities in Romania offer programs taught in English, making it accessible for international students who may not be fluent in Romanian.

Clinical Experience

Romanian medical programs prioritize practical training, clinical experience, internships, and research, preparing students for successful medical careers.

Multicultural Environment

Romanian universities offer a multicultural learning environment for international students, promoting personal and academic growth.

Romania offers a diverse array of medical study programs that cater to a wide range of student interests.
These include:
  • General Medicine: This comprehensive 6-year program equips students with the requisite knowledge and practical skills for a successful career in medicine.
  • Dentistry: This 6-year field dedicated to oral health provides in-depth knowledge about dental diseases, their prevention, and treatment techniques.
  • Pharmacy: This 5-year program provides a solid foundation in pharmaceutical sciences, preparing students for a career in pharmaceutical research and development.
  • Veterinary Medicine: This 6-year program is ideal for animal lovers as it prepares students for a career in animal healthcare and disease prevention.

    These programs are globally recognized and are designed to meet the highest international standards. They are taught in English, French, or Romanian, depending on the university. Apart from theoretical knowledge, students gain hands-on clinical experience, which is crucial in the medical profession.
    • Romania offers medical programs taught in multiple languages to cater to a global student body.
  • English Programs: Many universities offer English-taught programs, drawing a large number of international students. These programs ensure that language doesn't hinder quality medical education.
  • French Programs: There are also programs taught in French, making Romania an ideal choice for French-speaking students or those with French as a second language.
  • Romanian Programs: For full cultural immersion, programs taught in Romanian are available. International students typically undergo a year-long preparatory Romanian language course before beginning their studies.

    • All programs, regardless of the language of instruction, deliver high-quality medical education.

    Why Study Medicine in Romania with EuroMed?

    Globally Recognized Degrees:

    Romanian medical universities comply with the European Union's standards, ensuring a top-notch education. Their degrees are globally recognized, enabling graduates to practice medicine worldwide. With EuroMed, students are guided to Romania's best medical programs, equipping them for their career goals.

    Global recognition of degrees includes listing in the World Health Organization's World Directory of Medical Schools , and approval by the Medical Council of the European Union. This allows graduates to practice in all WHO and EU member states, respectively.
    Please note , local licensing exams may be required depending on where you plan to practice. This global acceptance underscores the worth of your educational investment.

    High-Quality Education:

    Romania's medical education adheres to high-quality standards with our partner universities boasting modern facilities and expert faculty. The comprehensive curriculum provides a well-rounded understanding of medicine, updated to reflect latest advancements.

    We go beyond theory; students gain practical experience via internships, clinical rotations, and research initiatives. This exposure prepares them for a successful career in medicine, fostering competent, ethical, and compassionate healthcare professionals.

    Affordability and Cost-effectiveness:

    Studying medicine in Romania is a cost-effective alternative compared to other European countries or the U.S., offering lower tuition fees and living expenses without compromising on education quality.

    Tuition fees in Romania range from €5,000 to €8,500 per year for international students, quite affordable compared to the UK. Moreover, Romania's cost of living is significantly lower, covering expenses like accommodation, food, transportation, and personal expenses. As per Numbeo, maintaining a standard of life in Bucharest requires about £2,300, whereas in London it requires around £4,700.

    We're dedicated to helping you find the most affordable options and managing your budget effectively while ensuring a top-tier education.

    Cultural Diversity and Rich Student Life:

    Romania's universities attract students from various countries, fostering an inclusive and multicultural environment. Students can enjoy Romania's rich history, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cities, providing a unique and enriching cultural experience. Romanian people are known for their hospitality, making international students feel welcome and well-integrated into the community. EuroMed offers comprehensive support in settling down and adapting to the new environment, ensuring you have the best possible experience during your studies.

    • International Students: According to the Romanian National Institute of Statistics, Romania welcomed around 30,000 international students from more than 100 countries in the academic year 2020-2021. This number has been consistently growing over the past decade.
    • Erasmus Programme: Romania participates in the Erasmus+ programme, which is a testament to its commitment to international student exchange and promoting cultural diversity.

    Excellent Post-Graduation Opportunities:

    Romania's medical degrees are recognized across the European Union and other countries, opening doors to various job opportunities and further specialization. Graduates from Romanian medical schools have a high success rate in securing residencies and jobs in their home countries and internationally.
    The hands-on training and rigorous curriculum equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in their medical careers.

    EuroMed is committed to guiding you through your journey and helping you achieve your aspirations in the medical field.

    High Standard of Living and Safety:

    Romania, known for its high standard of living, provides a perfect balance of affordable living and quality amenities. Its cost of living, much lower than many Western European nations, is particularly favorable for students.

    Key features include affordable housing, reasonably priced food, transport, and leisure activities, allowing for an extended budget capacity. Also, Romania maintains impressively low crime rates, ranking among the top 30% safest countries as per the Global Peace Index.

    The nation's thriving economy, largely driven by the IT, automotive, and manufacturing sectors, ensures a high standard of living. Romanians are notably hospitable and often fluent in English, aiding in a smoother transition for international students.

    Romania's universal healthcare system ensures accessible healthcare for students from EU/EEA countries, provided they have an EHIC. Non-EU/EEA students can equally access healthcare services by purchasing health insurance.
